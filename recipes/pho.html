How to Make Pho

Pho is a soul-warming noodle soup.

This Vietnamese dish balances deep, savory complexity with vibrant freshness. Though many would point to the noodles as its primary ingredient, its heart is in the fragrant, spice-infused broth.

You can buy a quick & simple kit at the local grocery store just about anywhere, but traditionally, the broth is simmered for hours with charred ginger, onions, and star anise, and topped with fresh basil, lime, chili, and crunchy bean sprouts.
